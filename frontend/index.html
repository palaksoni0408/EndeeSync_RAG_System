Common Questions

Is my data sent to the cloud?

Your vector store runs entirely locally via Endee. Document vectors never leave your machine. However, the final generation step uses OpenAI (cloud) by default, but you can configure local LLMs (Llama/Mistral) for full privacy.

What file formats are supported?

Currently, we support PDF, Markdown (.md), and plain text (.txt) files. The smart chunker automatically handles formatting and metadata retention for these formats.

How fast is the retrieval?

Endee's HNSW implementation achieves sub-millisecond latency for similarity search, even with millions of vectors. The total response time is primarily dominated by the LLM generation step.

How It Works

A seamless pipeline from raw documents to intelligent answers.

1

Ingest & Chunk

Documents are loaded, cleaned, and split into semantic chunks (512 tokens) with smart overlap to preserve context.

2

Embed & Store

Chunks are converted to 384-dimensional vectors using `all-MiniLM-L6-v2` and stored in Endee Vector DB with metadata.

3

Retrieve & Generate

Endee performs fast similarity search. The retrieved context is passed to the LLM (GPT-4/3.5) to generate a grounded answer.
